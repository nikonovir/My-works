My favourite speedcuber

Felix Zemdegs

He started solving the Rubik's Cube at the age of 12, in 2008, and after just 71 days he dropped below 20 seconds on the average time of 12 attempts and did not stop there. On January 30, 2010, he became the first human to officially become a sub-10 (less than 10 seconds) average time with a score of 9.21.
